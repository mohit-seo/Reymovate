Multi-City Local Service Business

Local SEO · Lead Generation · GMB Optimization
Local SEO

Challenge: A home services business operating across multiple cities had near-zero organic visibility. Each city branch had no dedicated web presence and was losing leads to local competitors.

Strategy: Built a complete local SEO architecture — individual city landing pages optimized for geo-specific keywords, GMB profiles optimized per location, local citation building, and a structured review acquisition strategy.

CASE STUDY 02

E-Commerce Store — 500+ Products

Technical SEO · Category Optimization · Schema
E-commerce SEO

Challenge: 500+ product store with decent traffic but poor conversion. A technical audit revealed crawl issues, thin category pages, duplicate descriptions, and missing schema suppressing rich results.

Strategy: Full Screaming Frog crawl audit, resolved redirect chains, implemented product and breadcrumb schema, rewrote category pages with buyer-intent keywords, restructured internal linking.

Outcome: Rich results appeared in SERPs. Organic traffic to category pages showed consistent growth over the following quarter with measurable improvements in organic revenue contribution.

How much do SEO services cost in Delhi?
SEO pricing at Reymovate depends on your goals, competition and the number of keywords and pages targeted. We offer one-time SEO audits, monthly retainers and project-based local SEO packages. Share your website and target keywords to get a custom quote and a free audit.
How long does SEO take to show results?
Most websites start seeing movement within 8 to 12 weeks, with compounding results from month 4 to 6 onwards. Local SEO and Google Business Profile optimization can show faster wins, while competitive national keywords take longer. SEO is a durable, long-term channel rather than an overnight result.
What is included in your SEO services?
Every engagement includes a technical SEO audit, on-page optimization, keyword and content strategy, local SEO and Google Business Profile, internal linking and ongoing reporting. We also handle AI SEO (AEO and GEO) so your brand appears in ChatGPT, Perplexity and Google AI Overviews.
Do you offer Local SEO and Google Business Profile optimization in Delhi?
Yes. Local SEO and Google Business Profile (GMB) optimization are core services. We optimize your profile, build local citations, target city and area keywords, and help you rank in the Google Map Pack across Delhi, Gurgaon and Delhi NCR.
